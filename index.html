Stages of work

After signing the contract on non-disclosure of information to third parties, work begins on the following stages.

Contract Signing

Signing a contract, liability of the parties, force majeure, conditions of sale and shipping, etc.

Prepayment

Prepayment by the client of the specified amount in the contract for the product.

Product Volume Accumulation

Accumulation of the product (gold, silver) in the amount specified in the contract. Product quality check by specialized companies (SGS).

Shipping

Shipping of the product to the client (self-delivery of the product by the client). Transfer of all necessary documents and certificates.
